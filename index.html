What is Iowa Girls Code Camp?

IGCC is the first ever coding camp for high school girls in the state of Iowa. The goal is to educate high school girls about careers in Computer Science and show them how to get started.

Is it free to attend?

Yes, the event is totally free to attend, including breakfast and lunch. Simply register here.

Do I need prior coding experience?

Nope! This event is geared toward total beginners, to show how easy it is to get started coding and all of the cool things you can make.
