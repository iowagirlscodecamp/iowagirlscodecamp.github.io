About

Iowa Girls Code Camp is the first ever coding camp for high school girls in the state of Iowa.
While Computer Science is one of the fastest growing majors today,
women make up only 0% of graduates.

Our goal is to educate young Iowa women about careers in Computer Science and give them the skills to start coding on their own.

FAQ

Is it free?

Yes, the event is totally free to attend, and includes breakfast and lunch.
Simply register here.

Do I need prior coding experience?

Nope! Iowa Girls Code Camp is geared toward total beginners, to show how easy it is to get started coding.

What do I need to bring?

- If you have a Windows or Mac laptop, please bring it with its power cord. If you don’t, we’ll have one for you to use.

- A pen and a favorite notebook to take notes if you want.

- An open mind and a willingness to try something new and learn!

Who’s eligible to attend?

We’re designing the program for high school girls, currently in 9th through 12th grade, but we’ll accept slightly younger or older girls who are motivated to learn to code!
